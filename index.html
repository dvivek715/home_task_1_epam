Strikes Hit Russian-Occupied Ukraine

posted on 29 june,2019 //Category, War

Explosions shook Russian-occupied cities in southern Ukraine, injuring a police chief and hitting a military facility, while a Russian missile strike killed at least two people and injured 29 others in the eastern city of Slovyansk, Ukrainian officials said. The explosions on Monday came as front lines in the east and south were largely deadlocked.

India Records 3,016 New Covid Cases, Highest In Nearly 6 Months

posted on 29 june,2019 //Category, Health

India recorded 3,016 new Covid cases in past 24 hours, a 40 per cent jump since yesterday. The daily positivity rate has been recorded at 2.7 per cent and the weekly positivity rate at 1.71 per cent, according to the Union health ministry data. Thursday's infection count is the highest in nearly six months. India had reported 3,375 cases on October 2 last year.
